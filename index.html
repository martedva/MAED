Restaurant No. 2

Nicolai Eigtveds Gade 32, 1402 København K

On the 30th of October 2017, 7.30PM, Laura and I visited Restaurant No.2 to celebrate our half year anniversary. I had been looking forward to visiting this particular restaurant since I had a couple of drinks there on the 15th of October 2016. The menu of the night was as follows:

Appetizer
2 servings of the snacks of the evening.
Entrées
Salted scallops on smoked cheese, kohlrabi with mussels sauce on burnt hay.
Fried zander with celeriac and smoked butter sauce.
Main Course
Grilled mallard with beets, mushrooms and aronia berries.
Dessert
Ice cream on roasted hazelnuts with danish apples and sour herbs.

"Vores plan har aldrig været at lave en parisisk bistro i København. Nu er vi her, hvor vi er, så vi laver en nordisk bistro i norden. Det hænger bedre sammen."

Christian Aarø i Magasinet AOK
